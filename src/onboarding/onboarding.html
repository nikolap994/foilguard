Active — you're protected

Your browser is now defended against phishing domains.

FoilGuard watches every site you visit and blocks dangerous impostors before they load — silently, locally, without sending your browsing data anywhere.

🔤
Typosquatting

Catches domains one or two letters off from real brands — like gogle.com or rnicrosoft.com.

🔢
Digit substitution

Detects leet-speak tricks like g00gle.com or paypa1.com that replace letters with numbers.

🔡
Look-alike characters

Spots Cyrillic, Greek, and other scripts that look identical to English letters in a browser address bar.

⛓️
Combosquatting

Blocks domains that combine a real brand with deceptive words — like paypal-login.com or amazon-security.com.

🕐
Newly registered domains

Flags brand-like domains that were registered in the last 30 days — a strong indicator of a phishing campaign.

🔒
Completely private

All detection runs in your browser. No account. No API key. No browsing history sent anywhere.

Legitimate sites are never blocked.

mail.google.com, accounts.paypal.com, login.microsoft.com — subdomains of real brands always score 0.
